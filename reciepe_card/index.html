Chicken Dumplings Reciepe
In a world full of trends, be a momo lover.

Ingredients For Chicken Dumplings:

  • For dough:
    • 2 cups Maida
    • 1/2 tsp Salt
    • 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • For filling:
    • Chicken
    • 1 cup Carrot, grated
    • 1 tbsp Oil
    • 1/2 cup Onion, finely chopped
    • 1 tsp Garlic, chopped
    • 1 tsp Soya sauce
    • to taste Salt
    • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
    • Green chillies , finely chopped
    • Coriander

How to make Dumplings:

  1. Mix the maida, salt and baking powder and knead to a stiff dough with water.
  2. Heat oil and add the onion and garlic. Saute over high heat and add the chicken. Turn around over high heat till thawing.Then add carrot.
  3. Then mix the soya sauce, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Roll the dough thin (translucent) and cut into 4"-5" rounds.
  5. Take a round, wet edges and place some filling in the center.
  6. Bring edges together to cover the filling. Twist to seal and fill the rest in the same way.
  7. Steam for about 10 minutes and serve with momo chutney.
